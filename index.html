How to Play

Controls

  • Use WASD or Arrow Keys to move your character
  • Your character will automatically attack nearby enemies
  • Press Space to use special abilities when available
  • Collect experience orbs to level up and choose new abilities

Objective

Survive as long as possible against endless waves of monsters. The longer you survive, the stronger you become, but the monsters also grow in number and strength.

Collect power-ups, level up your abilities, and strategically navigate the map to maximize your survival time and achieve the highest score possible.

About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game that draws inspiration from classic roguelike titles while adding its own unique twist. Players must navigate through procedurally generated environments while fighting off waves of increasingly difficult monsters.

What sets Monster Survivors apart is its innovative progression system, allowing players to build their character in countless ways through ability combinations and strategic power-up selections. Each playthrough offers a different experience, ensuring hours of engaging gameplay.

The game features stunning visuals, immersive sound effects, and smooth controls that work seamlessly across both desktop and mobile devices. Whether you have a few minutes or hours to play, Monster Survivors offers an accessible yet deeply rewarding gaming experience.
